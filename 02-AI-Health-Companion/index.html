"I have fever and body pain"

What it does

Four ways the assistant supports you

Each feature targets a specific gap in everyday health management — built for people who need quick, reliable guidance without waiting for an appointment.

01 — Guidance

Symptom Checker

Describe how you feel in plain language. The assistant suggests possible causes and sensible next steps, always recommending professional care when needed.

02 — Memory

Medicine Reminders

Tell the assistant your medication schedule. It helps you keep track so doses aren't missed — a leading cause of poor treatment outcomes.

03 — Knowledge Base

Reliable Health Info

Answers are grounded in a curated set of health FAQs and guidelines, so responses stay accurate and consistent rather than generic.

04 — Habits

Lifestyle Tips

Get personalized suggestions on sleep, hydration, diet, and stress — small daily changes that add up to better long-term health.
